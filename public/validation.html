How to spot-check any number yourself

  1. Open the matching report for the property (General Ledger for cash, reserves, payables, income, capital; Rent Roll for occupancy and deposits).
  2. Find the account number or column named above.
  3. Read the ending balance (for ledger accounts) or apply the simple math shown.
  4. On the dashboard, click the card or table row — a panel slides open listing the exact transactions behind that number.
